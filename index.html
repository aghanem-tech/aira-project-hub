The 22x Productivity Gain: A Breakdown

The productivity gain is calculated by comparing the time required for a diligent manual workflow against the highly automated AiRA workflow for a single strategic account.

Manual Workflow

  • Internal Context Review:5-10 mins
  • External Research (News, LinkedIn):15-25 mins

AiRA Workflow

The High Cost of "Being Prepared"

Our current sales preparation process is inefficient and doesn't scale, creating three critical business challenges.

hourglass_top

The Time Sink

A diligent AM spends up to ~45 minutes on manual research for a single strategic account—digging through CRM, searching Google for news, and trying to piece it all together.

mail_lock

The Engagement Gap

Bulk email campaigns are inefficient. Their generic, one-size-fits-all messaging fails to resonate with client-specific needs, resulting in low reply rates and missed conversion opportunities.

rule

The Strategy Gap

Without a centralized intelligence tool, outreach quality is inconsistent. There is no standard, data-driven playbook, forcing each AM to reinvent the wheel, leading to missed signals and a reactive sales motion.

The AiRA Solution: Features & Capabilities

AiRA's core feature is the automated Strategic Overview, a comprehensive client brief generated on demand. Here's what's inside.

  • Automated Industry & Trend Analysis Identifies the client's specific industry and surfaces relevant, localized market trends.
  • Seat & Tech Stack Analysis Compares internal GWS seat counts to public headcount data and checks for competitors like Outlook.
  • Intelligent Stakeholder Mapping Uses targeted searches to find the names and likely titles for key decision-makers and influencers.
  • Deep Culture & Hiring Analysis Scrapes client websites to analyze company culture and identify key hiring trends and open roles.
  • Actionable Content Generation Creates hyper-personalized outreach emails, downloadable reports, and client-ready one-pagers.
  • Automated Workflow Tools Includes one-click actions to create quotes and add renewal reminders to Google Calendar.
